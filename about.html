Welcome to S-MART

S-MART super market is one of the leading supermarkets in Andhra Pradesh. It was started in the year 2019 and it is located in the heart of the city with ample car parking facility. All leading FMCG brands are available here. All products are sold at a discounted price. The Samkarathil group which runs the supermarket has over 70 years of retail experience. Only products which meet the stringent quality norms of the firm is available here. We also sell home grown vegetables. The supermarket has well trained and experienced staff to serve you.

S-Mart supermarket is a dynamic leader in the retail and food sector. An impeccable eye for quality and our continuing strive to provide exceptional service is what forms the core of S-Mart Supermarket. This commitment has led us to become one of the leading supermarkets in Kerala. S-Mart is a one stop shopping experience for all products that would fulfill all your household as well as personal needs,we're on a mission to deliver quality beyond question and convenience that adds something great to your day.
